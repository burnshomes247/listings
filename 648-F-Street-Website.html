Stunning Capitol Hill Rowhouse With In-Law Basement & Detached Carriage House

648 F Street offers three fully independent living spaces under one ownership — a rare configuration for Capitol Hill. The main house, basement in-law suite, and detached carriage house studio each function independently, making this property equally suited for multigenerational households, long-term guests, or rental income.

The main house spans two levels with three bedrooms and two and a half baths. The main level features nearly 9.5-foot ceilings, an open living and dining area with gas fireplace, and a gourmet kitchen with a 48" Thermador refrigerator, 36" six-burner Miele gas range, quartz countertops, and custom cabinetry. Upstairs, three bedrooms and two full baths occupy the entire floor, including a primary with walk-in closet. A pull-down attic adds storage rarely found in Capitol Hill rowhouses.

The basement in-law suite is fully self-contained with one bedroom, full bath, complete kitchen, in-unit laundry, and private front and rear entrances.

The detached carriage house includes a one-car conditioned garage — usable as parking, home office, or flex space — with a studio apartment above featuring a full bath, kitchenette, and private balcony. The owner held a DC short-term rental license for the studio.

Fully renovated in 2021, the property includes hardwood floors, crown molding, whole-house water filtration, new HVAC, leased solar panels, home speaker system, blackout shades, retractable screens, and a security and video surveillance system. Located on a quiet residential block within walking distance of the H Street Corridor, Eastern Market, Union Station, Union Market, Stanton Park, and multiple grocery stores. This unique residential compound represents a rare opportunity in a premier Capitol Hill location.
