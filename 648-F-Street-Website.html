Stunning Capitol Hill Rowhouse With In-Law Basement & Detached Carriage House

Spectacular modernized Capitol Hill classic rowhome with basement in-law suite for multigenerational living and detached carriage house with one car conditioned garage and upper level studio apartment — this is not to be missed! Built in 1911 and fully reimagined in 2020, 648 F Street NE delivers the scale, quality, and flexibility that rarely comes to market in this neighborhood. The property offers five total bedrooms and four and a half bathrooms across the main house, a fully independent basement in-law suite, and a detached carriage house with studio apartment above. The main level offers nearly 9.5 foot ceilings and centers on an open and modern living and dining flow anchored by a gas fireplace, flowing into a gourmet kitchen appointed with a 48" Thermador soft open/close refrigerator, 36" six-burner Miele gas range, quartz countertops, and custom cabinetry. A half bath and rear deck with patio access complete the main level.

Upstairs, three bedrooms and two full baths occupy the entire upper level, including a primary bedroom with a substantial walk-in closet. A newly created attic with pull-down stair access provides storage that is genuinely uncommon in Capitol Hill rowhouses of this era. Ceiling height is just under nine feet. The basement in-law suite is a fully independent living space with one bedroom, a full bath, complete kitchen, in-unit laundry, and both front and rear entrances — ideal for multigenerational living or long-term guests.

The detached carriage house features a one-car conditioned garage with its own mini-split HVAC — equally suited as a home office or flex space as it is for parking — and a studio apartment above with full bath, kitchenette, and private balcony. The owner has held a DC short-term rental license for the carriage house studio. The property is further equipped with leased solar panels, a whole-house water filtration system, new HVAC system, home speaker system, top-down/bottom-up blackout shades throughout, retractable screens, security system, and video surveillance. Crown molding, hardwood floors, and designer details are found throughout. Situated on a quiet residential block in the heart of Capitol Hill, 648 F Street NE is walkable to the H Street Corridor, Stanton Park, Eastern Market, Union Station, Union Market, two grocery stores, yoga studios, personal training gyms, independent coffee shops, and some of the best neighborhood bars and restaurants the city has to offer!
