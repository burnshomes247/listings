A Bethesda Classic

This charming and spacious home is in excellent condition and offers the best of Bethesda living. With 4 bedrooms and 3 full bathrooms, the layout is both comfortable and functional, featuring an updated kitchen, gleaming hardwood floors, and a cozy fireplace.

The finished basement adds valuable living space, while the welcoming front porch and 1-car detached garage with driveway round out the home's practical appeal. In-unit washer and dryer, dishwasher, central air, and gas heat make everyday living effortless.

The location is truly unbeatable — walkable to downtown Bethesda, the Bethesda Metro and Medical Center Metro stations, NIH, and Walter Reed National Military Medical Center. Top-rated schools serve the neighborhood, including Bethesda Elementary, Westland Middle School, and Bethesda-Chevy Chase High School.
