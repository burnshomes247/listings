The Perfect Bethesda Home

A beautifully appointed home featuring hardwood floors and crown molding throughout the main and upper levels, with wainscoting and board-and-batten detail in the foyer, living, and dining rooms. Decorative columns, a grand staircase with carpet runner and wood railing, and a formal dining room with wallpaper accent set a refined tone. The home office offers built-in floor-to-ceiling shelving behind French doors, while the mudroom delivers built-in cubbies and a butler's pantry/wet bar with wine refrigerator.

The open family room centers on a gas fireplace with white mantel and flows into a chef's kitchen with white shaker cabinets, quartz countertops, large island with a heavy-edge profile top, gas built-in cooktop, double wall ovens, decorative tile backsplash, and under-cabinet lighting — plus a breakfast area with bay window. Custom lights and moldings throughout. Upstairs includes the primary suite, which features a tray ceiling, multiple closets, an accent wall, and a spa bath with a freestanding soaking tub, heated floors, an oversized frameless glass shower with a rain head and bench, a double vanity, and pendant lighting.

Three secondary bedrooms, a jack-and-jill bath, an additional full bath, and a vaulted bonus room complete this level. The finished lower level offers a family rec room, bedroom, full bath, and a workout room with rubber flooring accessed through glass French doors. Outside, the Hardiplank siding, a covered front porch with columns and railing, an oversized front-loading two-car garage, and a metal porch roof deliver strong curb appeal, while solar panels and a Level 2 EV charger speak to modern efficiency.

The backyard is built for every season — a custom screened porch addition with four skylights, beadboard ceiling, recessed lighting, built-in speakers, and a Nana tri-fold wall opening to the family room transitions to a flagstone patio, raised stone gas-piped fire pit with seating wall, and a large, flat, privacy-fenced yard with mature landscaping.

Lone Oak offers a superior location with easy access to major commuter roads, is a short walk to Wildwood Shopping Center, and is also just minutes to Downtown Bethesda.
