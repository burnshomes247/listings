An Exceptional Opportunity

Welcome to Birchwood at Brambleton, Loudoun County's premier 55+ Active Adult community! This four-level, 3 bedroom, 2.5 bath townhome-style condo offers nearly 2,600 sq. ft. of living space with the convenience of a private elevator to all levels. The open main level features 9-ft ceilings, hardwood floors, and a gourmet kitchen with granite countertops, stainless steel appliances, a walk-in pantry, a central island, and access to a personal deck.

The entire third floor is devoted to the luxurious primary suite, complete with dual vanities, a soaking tub, oversized shower, in-unit laundry, and two large storage closets. Upstairs, two spacious bedrooms share a full bath, while the entry level offers a versatile mudroom and private garage. Birchwood at Brambleton's resort-style amenities are steps away and include indoor and outdoor pools, a clubhouse, fitness center, pickleball and bocce courts, a theater room, golf simulator, walking trails, a lake with fishing pier, community garden, and a full calendar of social activities. The HOA fee includes Verizon Fios cable and internet base package!

Ideally located near Brambleton Town Center, shopping, dining, healthcare, the Silver Line Metro, and Dulles Airport — this home checks every box!
