Rosslyn's Prestigious Highgate Community

1544 N Colonial Terrace is a four-level brick townhome in Rosslyn's prestigious Highgate community, offering 3 bedrooms, 2.5 baths, and an attached two-car garage.

The main living level features hardwood floors, crown molding, a gas fireplace, and a bay window overlooking the street. The eat-in kitchen has a center island with a 6-burner KitchenAid gas cooktop, dark granite countertops, rich cherry cabinetry, double wall oven, and a breakfast nook.

The primary suite spans its own level with hardwood floors and a bay window. Storage is exceptional — an oversized walk-in closet and a second walk-in provide serious wardrobe space. The en suite bath includes a freestanding soaking tub, glass-enclosed shower with travertine tile and built-in bench, dual vessel sinks, and a dedicated makeup station.

Two additional bedrooms and a full bath occupy the top floor. The lower level has a recreation room, laundry, and direct access to a private flagstone patio.

Major systems updated: roof replaced 2020, both HVAC units replaced 2020, windows replaced 2024, hot water heater replaced within the past six months.

Just 0.3 miles to Rosslyn Metro and under a mile to Georgetown, with easy access to dining, shopping, riverfront trails, and DCA airport.
